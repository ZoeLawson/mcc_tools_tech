Health Factors

A health factor is any practice that you engage in, or situation to which you are exposed, that may influence your health. The following list is by no means complete but should give you a good starting point in what to record in your journey to health.

Recording any of the following health factors in your journal may help you to determine what is helpful with your symptoms and what is not. Please consult with a medical professional about any of these practices.

Please note that this is not a complete list!  This is simply to give you an idea on the types of things you may want to track and record to determine if and how they are effecting your health.

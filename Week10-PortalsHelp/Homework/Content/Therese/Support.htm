Suicide Prevention

Sadly, the highest percentages of suicides are amongst the chronic pain and illness populations.  If you feel at risk, PLEASE reach out!

If you are in crisis, call 911 immediately!

 

National Suicide Prevention Hotline: 1-800-273-8255

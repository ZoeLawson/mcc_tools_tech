Accessibility

Not just for folks with disabilities

Try using a computer with bifocals.

What happens when you break your wrist or thumb skiing?

Making sure your content is accessible helps everyone.

Accessible Web content

https://www.w3.org/standards/webdesign/accessibility

  • Alternate text for images
  • CC or Transcripts for audio/video
  • Tables can be read by screen readers
  • High contrast and no color-blind issues (and no seizures)
  • Possible to navigate without a mouse

Alt Text

Most images should have it, unless decorative or explained inline.

Give a brief description of the image. Be clear, be succinct, provide context.

Icons should describe what the thing does, not what it is.

- Save not Save icon

Click Save (). doesn't need alt text because the text gives the context.

Writing for translation

Use simple English.

Avoid contractions, slang, and idioms.

Watch out for assumed subjects and objects.

Be aware of "variables"

Be aware of conditional text.

Variables and translation

Don't add s or 's to variables.

For example:

Renders as:
Click Create to add Colors.

Conditions and translation

When you create a translation, the smallest unit you should make conditional is a sentence.

Just because you can add a phrase in English, doesn't mean that how the grammar works in another language.

Possessives often change word order.

Verbs go in different places.

Metadata

Information about information

Helps with SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

A good CMS uses metadata to help organize (and maintain) information

Can automate content creation

Middlesex Community College - Tools and Technologies for Tech Writers - Winter 2023

Common types of metadata

Hashtags, tag clouds

Important dates: Creation, last edited, last reviewed, publish date, expiration date, review date

Publisher information: Owner, publisher, author, copyright dates

Audience information: Novice/Expert, User/Administrator, Internal/External

Keywords: Index terms, Glossary terms, Important concepts

Subject area: Used for filters, authorization, etc.
